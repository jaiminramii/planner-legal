Frequently Asked Questions

How does Quick Add work?

Type a task in plain English — like "gym at 6pm for 1h" or "call mom tomorrow at 10" — and Kronday schedules it automatically, no pickers or menus needed.

How do reminders work?

Kronday can notify you before a task starts. You can set how many minutes in advance in Settings. Notifications are generated entirely on your device.

Does Kronday sync across my devices?

Yes — enable iCloud Sync in Settings to keep your tasks up to date across all your devices signed in with the same Apple ID.

Can I set up recurring tasks?

Yes. When creating or editing a task, choose a repeat option (daily, weekly, or custom days) and Kronday will keep adding it to your timeline automatically.

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed and managed entirely through Apple. Go to iPhone Settings → your name → Subscriptions to view, change, or cancel your plan.

How do I delete my data?

Deleting the app removes all locally stored tasks. If you used iCloud Sync, you can also remove Kronday's data from iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Avani Rami ("we", "us", or "our") handles your information when you use the Planner app ("the App"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy applies worldwide to all users of the App.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. The App operates entirely on your device.

The only data the App creates is:

  • Tasks and schedules — created by you, stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.
  • App preferences — such as notification settings and reminder times, stored locally on your device.

2. iCloud Sync

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your task data is synced across your devices through your personal iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy. We never have access to your iCloud data.

3. Notifications

If you grant notification permission, the App schedules local notifications on your device as reminders for your tasks. These notifications are generated entirely on-device and no notification data is sent to any external server.

4. Subscriptions and Payments

All payments and subscription management are handled entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect, process, or store any payment information. Your billing details are governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not use tools such as Google Analytics, Facebook SDK, or any similar service.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

7. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account, security is managed by your device's built-in protections and Apple's security infrastructure. We recommend keeping your device and iCloud account secure with a strong passcode and two-factor authentication.

8. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the App from your device. If you use iCloud sync, you can also delete the App's iCloud data from your iPhone Settings → Apple ID → iCloud → Manage Storage.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
